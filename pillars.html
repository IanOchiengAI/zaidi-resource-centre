OUR PILLARS

Education & Training

Training teachers/educators, healthcare professionals of all cadres, religious/spiritual practitioners and other relevant stakeholders on muscular dystrophy and other disabilities. Building capacity across communities to ensure dignity and access for all.

Research

Bridging knowledge gaps on muscular dystrophy and disability using feminist and decolonial approaches. Prioritising lived experiences of disability or having loved ones with disabilities and centering community voices in our work.

Wholeness

Mind, body, and spirit wellness. Focus on play, grief and mourning, music, and dance. Creating spaces for joy, healing, and authentic expression.
